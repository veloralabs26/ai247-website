Score your readiness

Eighteen checks across four areas. Count your ticks, your score is on the next page.

1. Processes

We can name 3+ repetitive tasks that eat time every week.
Those tasks follow clear, repeatable steps (not pure judgement calls).
We know roughly how many hours each one takes per month.
The same work is done the same way each time, regardless of who does it.

2. Data & tools

Our customer/lead information lives in a system (CRM or sheet), not just inboxes and heads.
We mostly use modern tools that can connect to others (have logins / integrations).
Our data is reasonably clean and up to date.
We know where each important piece of information currently lives.
We use WhatsApp, email, or a website form to talk to customers.

3. Team & ownership

Someone will own the rollout and answer questions.
The team is open to changing how a few tasks get done.
Leadership wants to free up time for growth, not just cut costs.

4. Goals & budget

We have a clear goal (more leads, faster replies, less admin).
We can measure success with a number or two.
We're ready to start small and expand what works.
We have a modest budget set aside to build the first automation.
We'd rather fix a real bottleneck than chase the shiniest tool.
We can commit a little time to testing over the first few weeks.
