What separates a system that works

  • Relevance over volume. 200 well-targeted, personalised messages beat 5,000 generic ones, every time.
  • Speed to lead. Reply in minutes, not hours. AI handles the instant first response around the clock.
  • Follow-up persistence. Most replies come after the 3rd touch. Automate the sequence so it always happens.
  • One source of truth. Every lead, message, and status lives in one CRM so nothing slips through.
  • Measure and tune. Track reply rate, booking rate, and cost per call, then double down on what works.
A realistic benchmark

A well-run AI outbound system commonly lands a 5–15% positive reply rate, several times typical cold benchmarks, because every message is relevant and every follow-up actually happens.
