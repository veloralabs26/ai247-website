Do it right

How to roll it out safely

  • Feed it your real answers. The assistant should learn from your FAQs, past chats, and policies, not guess.
  • Keep a human in the loop. Let AI handle the routine and hand off anything sensitive or unusual to a person.
  • Set clear boundaries. Define what it can and can't promise, refunds, pricing, timelines, so it never oversteps.
  • Stay on-brand. Match your tone and language (including Arabic), so replies sound like you.
  • Review and improve weekly. Check transcripts, fix gaps, and the system gets sharper over time.
The payoff

Businesses that automate first-response typically cut reply times from hours to seconds and free their team from a large share of repetitive tickets, without sacrificing the personal touch on what matters.
