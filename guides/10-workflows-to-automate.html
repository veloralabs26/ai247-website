10 Workflows You Can Automate
Workflows 1–5

Start with the time-eaters

These five are the ones almost every business should automate first, they're repetitive, rule-based, and happen all day long.

1

Lead capture & routing

Every enquiry (form, DM, WhatsApp) flows into one place, tagged, timestamped, and assigned to the right person automatically.

2

Instant first reply

A warm auto-response goes out within minutes so a lead never sits unanswered, the single biggest cause of lost deals.

3

Follow-up sequences

Automated, personalised follow-ups over days or weeks, so no lead goes cold and no one has to remember to chase.

4

Appointment scheduling

Bookings confirm, remind, and reschedule themselves. No back-and-forth, far fewer no-shows.

5

Quote & proposal generation

Pull client details and pricing into a branded quote in seconds instead of rebuilding the same document every time.

Workflows 6–10

Then scale the back office

6

Invoice & payment reminders

Invoices send themselves and gently chase late payers, improving cash flow without awkward calls.

7

Data entry between tools

Stop copying information from one app to another. Let it sync automatically and accurately.

8

AI inbox triage

Incoming messages get summarised, categorised, and routed, your team opens their inbox already sorted.

9

Review & reputation requests

Happy customers get an automatic nudge to leave a review at the perfect moment.

10

Weekly reporting

Your key numbers are gathered and delivered to your inbox on a schedule, no spreadsheets required.

How to choose

Don't do all ten at once. Pick the one that wastes the most time this week, automate it, then use the time you save to build the next.
