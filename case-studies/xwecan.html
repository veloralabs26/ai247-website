XWECAN

PR agency serving global tech firms, public figures, and politicians
Outcomes
  • We secured placements in CNN, Forbes, WSJ, Fortune, BBC and dozens of tier-1 outlets.
  • We achieved an 18% positive-reply rate—4-5× typical cold-PR benchmarks.
  • We reduced account-management headcount by 80% while delivering more wins per client.
Challenge

XWECAN's high-touch PR campaigns required large account teams and manual research. Scaling to tens of thousands of hyper-personalized pitches daily was impractical with their existing workflow.

Solution
  • We auto-generated pitch drafts from client onboarding summaries, emphasizing the angles most relevant to each media segment.
  • We built a research engine that scraped recent articles, identified journalists by beat and publication, and enriched records with email, LinkedIn, X, Muck Rack, and past coverage links.
  • We set up 100+ rotating sender identities enabling >10,000 personalized emails/day with sequenced follow-ups.
  • We deployed GPT-powered icebreakers that referenced each journalist's latest work, producing intros that routinely earned "best pitch I've seen" replies.
  • We automated media-hit tracking, Qwoted/HARO matching, and project-management workflows, cutting admin to near-zero.
